I am Beatriz Paulino and I am a rising junior who graduates high school in 2018. I was born in Dominican Republic and I was raised in Washington D.C. I moved to Lynn, Massachusetts with my mother once my parents were separated. As a child I have been curious and afraid to speak up. I am still like that despite my attempts to try not to be unfortunately. At a young age I have pushed myself to be smart and do my best in school. I have been an honor student since middle school. My life used to be just school and home, but now I managed to do volunteer work and hang out more with my friends. I'm a typically shy person around new people which I am trying to fix.

I am a very avid reader, my reading ranges from fantasy to historical fiction. I can basically read any book genre, and my preference depends on my mood. I can read quite fast usually finishing abook in a couple of hours or even months. I enjoy watching Game of Thrones, Girl meets World, korean dramas, anime, and even cartoons at time. I like to write poetry at times or doodle. I'm quite fond of kickboxing and volleyball. I like volunteering at the senior center but recently I haven't due to my upcoming schedule.

This is a strange one. But who I am right is not who I desire to be. I want to be this outspoken persom who isn't bothered by what others think and will stand up for her rights and others. I want to be confident in what I do, feel, and say. This is one of my struggles as a person. It may seem childish but I want to contribute a big change in our society, but I have to overcome my fear. That is who I wish to be and who I hope I grow up to be.
